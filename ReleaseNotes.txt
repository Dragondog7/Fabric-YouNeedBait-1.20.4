 ## Fabric 0.3.2.0
 ### New

 - New custom fishing rod with 3d model

 - Shift Right-click while holding in main hand opens the interface
 - Modifier slot (green) - Glow Berries (Experience bonus after each catch)

 - Where you clean your fish
 - Requires a filet knife
 - There is a 10% chance for bonus loot
